"Why do I need a new desktop?"
  1. I want to work on better projects with better hardware.
  2. I do not want to risk this laptop getting a lowered life expectancy because of intensive projects.
  3. I want to pursue more things like 3D, programming, game development, and etc., which is something this laptop cannot handle well without any risks.
  4. I want to make more money.

What are the specs needed for the desktop?

Any PC made for gaming will do, as stronger PCs are essential for things like video editing, 3D, and etc. Gaming PCs tend to fit this mark as they have higher RAM, better CPU power, and a better GPU for rendering and processing media smoothly. 1TB or higher storage would be good as well to ensure it lasts as long as it can.
